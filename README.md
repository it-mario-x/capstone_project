#### Executive summary
This analysis is to determine what are the factors that can be responsible for good or bad performance in maths tests for a group of high school students

#### Rationale
This question is important because it allows to determine where we should pay attention for high school students to succeed. This is a very important question for us all as a society.

#### Research Question
What can determine a good or bad performance in mathematics for a group of (high-school) students.

#### Data Sources
We will use a data set from Kaggle
https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rkiattisak/student-performance-in-mathematics


#### Methodology
We will use a GridSearchCV with a Lasso regression

#### Results
The results show that the most important factor to take into account for a good performance in maths is to hava good lunch.
